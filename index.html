About Us

We all came together with this idea in Las Vegas. While in a casino, a man collapsed — later we learned it was due to a smoking-related condition. That experience sparked PurePuff: a diffuser that mimics the experience of smoking but uses 100% natural scents like fruit to help people quit. Since then, we’ve gained sponsors and gone worldwide.

Main Points

  • Reduces smoking and vaping addiction
  • Supports lung recovery
  • Available in different flavors/scents
